My Favorite Scientist of All Time!!

Albert Einstein was a pioneer in his way of thinking, he is best known for his Theory of Relativity. His mass–energy equivalence formula E = mc2, which arises from relativity theory, has been dubbed "the world's most famous equation".

"We cannot solve our problems with the same thinking we used when we created them."
